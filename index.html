HOW TRUTHSCAN WORKS
01
⬡
STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS
Detects format patterns like Watch:, Breaking:, (video) tags, censored words, and source attribution formats that statistically separate fake from real headlines.
02
◎
VOCABULARY SCORING
50+ words calibrated against the Kaggle fake news dataset. Emotional words like hilarious, wrecked, unhinged signal fake; institutional words like committee, legislation signal real.
03
◈
TF-IDF DATASET MATCH
Cosine similarity against 400+ verified fake/real entries. Top-5 matches cast weighted votes. The more your text resembles known fake content, the higher the fake score.
04
◆
WEIGHTED VERDICT
All three scores are combined with calibrated weights (Structure ×1.0, Vocabulary ×0.8, Dataset ×0.6) to produce a final verdict with confidence percentage.
