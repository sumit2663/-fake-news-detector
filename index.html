HOW IT WORKS — 5 REAL VERIFICATION SOURCES
01
🔗
REAL ARTICLE FETCHING
Paste any news URL — TruthScan fetches the full article via allorigins.win CORS proxy, extracts the headline, body text, author, and publish date. Works on any public URL.
02
🏷
DOMAIN REPUTATION
Checks the article's domain against a database of 500+ known fake news, satire, and trusted sources compiled from media bias research (MBFC, NewsGuard categories).
03
📖
WIKIPEDIA CROSS-REFERENCE
Extracts key claims and entities, then queries Wikipedia's live API (prop=extracts) to retrieve actual article content and compare facts in real time.
04
📡
GDELT LIVE NEWS
Searches the GDELT Project — a live index of 10,000+ global news outlets. Finds real articles about the same topic to confirm or deny the story's existence in credible media.
05
🤖
CLAIMBUSTER AI
Sends each sentence to the ClaimBuster API — a real AI trained to score how "check-worthy" a claim is. High scores on multiple sentences indicate verifiable (or false) factual claims.
